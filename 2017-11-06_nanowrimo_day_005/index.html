NaNoWriMo - Day 005

6926

So… I wrote nothing yesterday. I had things to do, bla bla bla…

I hope that this is the first and last time that this happens, at least this month. The 1667 words are at my limit, both in creative terms and in time available, so if I fail to meet my goal in one day, it becomes really hard to catch up again…

NaNoWriMo is stressful, but that’s the point, you need to prove to yourself that you can do it!

I really want to finish the 50000 words by the end of the month, wish me luck!
